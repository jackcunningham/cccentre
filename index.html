Some frequently asked questions...

+My car has been damaged. What should I do?
If you haven't already, call your insurer. They'll give you a list of providers. If you decide to use us, we'll call you within an hour to arrange the work.
+How do I avoid losing my no claims bonus.
You might find you are within the maximum of claims you can make. Check with your insurer and you might find you can keep your no claims bonus.
+Which insurance companies are you approved accident repair specialists for?
We are approved repairers for Island Insurance and National Farmers Union.
+How do I know I'll get a good repair on my car?
We carry the BSI, PAS 125 kitemark™ for Vehicle Damage Repair. You can have confidence in the quality of repair and trust that the vehicle will be repaired safely and to the highest standards.
